Miêu tả

Immerse yourself in football with the all-new GOAL app. Faster than an in-form Kyle Walker, GOAL brings you all the breaking news and the latest scores from the world’s best leagues and biggest tournaments. If football is your everything, then GOAL is the only app you need.

Personalise your passion with the ‘My Feed’ feature and curate an experience tailored to your football fandom. Simply choose your favourite teams and the competitions that matter most to you, and GOAL will ensure that those headlines are the ones you’ll hear first.

NEWS
GOAL correspondents bring you closer to clubs and players with unrivalled coverage from the all over the world.

LIVESCORES
GOAL has the fastest live score functionality in football. Across every match, league and tournament, domestic or international, if someone scores, you can be sure to know about it.

COMPREHENSIVE MATCH DATA
The details make the difference. Which is why GOAL is bringing you the most extensive statistics live from the pitch; including line-ups, text commentary, table updates and much more.

HEAD-TO-HEAD
Every day is a derby day and we have the data for that ‘must win’ date in your diary. Use GOAL’s head-to-head feature to see how your team has fared against any team in the fixture list.

INSTANT NOTIFICATIONS
Everything in football, all in one place. Get all this and much much more with instant notifications. Register to join the conversation and become a fan of the fastest app in football.
