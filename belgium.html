FFF (Men's Team) 2024/25 Stadium Home

The FFF 2024 Home kit takes inspiration from classic France shirts of the 1960s while also celebrating the vibrant and diverse culture of the country. An oversized Cockerel on the chest and the tri-colour French flag ribbing leave no doubt as to which country this shirt represents.



A blue polo collar, vibrant crest against a textured white shirt and a single white star elevate this modern home shirt. On the back of the collar, you'll find a playful interpretation of the flag of St George in red and blue. It's a fun flavour to inspire and unite players and fans alike around the game.
